# Ralph Lauren Corporation (ralph-lauren-corporation)

Ralph Lauren Corporation (NYSE: RL) is a global premium-lifestyle company founded by Ralph Lauren in 1967 and headquartered in New York City. The company designs, markets, and distributes apparel, accessories, home, and fragrances across a portfolio of brands including Polo Ralph Lauren, Ralph Lauren Collection, Ralph Lauren Purple Label, Double RL (RRL), Lauren Ralph Lauren, and Chaps, along with Polo Golf, RLX, Pink Pony, Ralph Lauren Home and Paint, Ralph Lauren Watches and Fine Jewelry, and Ralph Lauren Restaurants. Direct-to-consumer commerce runs on Adobe Commerce, and category-extension brands are operated through licensing partners (eyewear via Luxottica, fragrances via L'Oréal). The corporation operates retail stores in dozens of countries with no publicly documented developer APIs, partner program portal, affiliate API, or open OpenAPI/Postman/GraphQL surface as of the profiling date.

## Brand Portfolio

- **Polo Ralph Lauren** — signature, timeless sportswear and tailored clothing in the classic American style.
- **Ralph Lauren Collection** — women's runway and luxury ready-to-wear.
- **Ralph Lauren Purple Label** — men's luxury and made-to-measure tailoring.
- **Double RL (RRL)** — heritage Americana, workwear, denim, and vintage-inspired pieces.
- **Lauren Ralph Lauren** — accessible-luxury lifestyle collections for women.
- **Chaps** — classic American style updated for modern lifestyles.
- **Polo Golf / RLX Golf** — performance and golf apparel.
- **Pink Pony** — cause-marketing brand benefiting cancer programs.
- **Ralph Lauren Home and Paint** — home furnishings, textiles, and paint.
- **Ralph Lauren Watches and Fine Jewelry** — Swiss-made watches and jewelry.
- **Ralph Lauren Restaurants** — Polo Bar, Ralph's, The Bar at Ralph Lauren and related hospitality.
